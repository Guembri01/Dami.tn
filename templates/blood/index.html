About us


Dami is a tunisian association who manage blood donation.The association founded in 2022 is headquartered in tunisia, and has a national scope


Feel good about your decision! Dami sets standards to ensure patient and donor safety. Accredited facilities undergo a rigorous assessment to prove they meet Dami's high standards, which ensure optimal safety for donors and patients.


Our Values


safety: We promote safe blood and blood components (red cells, platelets and plasma) use and supply;

Commitment: Our conduct is patient and donor centred, as well as professional;

Commitment: Our conduct is innovative and culturally sensitive;

Respect: We respect our Members, Participants, as well as the blood donors, patients, health professionals and providers that we serve;

Integrity: Our conduct is credible and transparent.
